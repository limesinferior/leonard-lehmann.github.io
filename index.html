Personal information

Being a Student of Information Systems Engineering which is a Mixture between Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at the TU Dresden, I became interested in a wide range of different topics during my studies. Starting from Web-Development and what it means to think in terms of User-Experience to implementing a Kalman-Filter in Matlab for GPS-Sensor data fusion or the development of a therapy-decision support system I developed Software Solutions using a variety of languages like Java, Python, C, Javascript or Hardware-Description Languages like VHDL.

I personally think that an essence of modern day engineering is to think out-of-the-box, in other words the ability to connect different disciplines together to find solutions. While in the past it was necessary to be good in your discipline of f.e. being a mechanical engineer, the ability of prpgramming has become a key asset in any field of engineering.
